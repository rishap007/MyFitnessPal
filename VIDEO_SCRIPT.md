## ğŸ¯ **PRO TIPS FOR RECORDING**

### **Voice:**
1. Speak clearly and enthusiastically
2. Smile while talking (it comes through in voice)
3. Pause briefly between sections
4. Use varied tone - not monotone
5. Re-record any sections that feel off

### **Mouse Movement:**
1. Move mouse smoothly and deliberately
2. Pause mouse when speaking about something
3. Circle important elements with cursor
4. Don't move mouse randomly

### **Pacing:**
1. Don't rush - give viewers time to see
2. Wait 1-2 seconds after clicking before speaking
3. Let animations complete before moving on
4. Pause for effect on impressive features
